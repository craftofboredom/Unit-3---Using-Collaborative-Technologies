I can describe the purposes for using collaborative technologies

There are a variety of technology that is used for business, personal and educational purposes. I’ve itemised a few examples below and the capabilities. Not all software offers all of the purposes mentioned. This can vary depending on the software and plan used.

Technology
Software Examples
Purpose
  • Zoom
  • Skype
  • Google Hangouts

Facilitates meetings for personal, work or learning. Some features allow a Webinar setup where participants interaction is controlled by a host.

  • Allows participants to communicate via video call
  • Has ‘Share screen’ capability which enables presenters to share their desktop to others
  • Participants can also chat to each other within the call
  • Has the ability to record meetings for future distribution
  • Adobe Connect

Facilitates a great learning environment from anywhere.
  • Allows the tutor to control speakers within the call.
  • Can share files to participants/learners
  • Has the ability to record lessons for future distribution
  • WhatsApp
  • Viber
  • Cisco Jabber

Allows the creation of group chats which enables multiple participants to collaborate in one message thread.

  • Allows the sharing of images and files within the chat
  • Allows audio and video calls
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Twitter

Allows the public to ‘follow’ or ‘befriend’ accounts. This is very useful for businesses, public figures and content creators to have a platform where a notification is sent to all their followers whenever new content is posted.
  • Can be used to setup personal and business accounts
  • Used for promoting business and having online presence for popular icons
  • Allows interaction between people by liking their posts
  • Google drive
  • Dropbox
  • Onedrive

Works as an online hard drive where people can collaborate, upload and download documents. This is useful for project collaboration.
  • Allows multiple people to access and edit files, sometimes simultaneously and instantaneously
